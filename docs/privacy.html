Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Kid Learn Quest is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our educational games. This policy explains how we handle information.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our games are designed for children ages 1-13. We comply with COPPA:

  • We do not require personal information to play
  • Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • We do not display behavioral advertising

3. Information We Collect

The web version stores only:

  • Game scores and levels (stored in your browser)
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted

4. Advertising

We may display child-safe advertisements. These ads do not track users or collect personal information.

5. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at: kidlearnquest.support@gmail.com
